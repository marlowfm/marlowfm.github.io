Our new mobile radio studio - a.k.a. 'the trailer' - made its debut at the Marlow Town Regatta & Festival on Saturday 13th June. The Mayor of Marlow, Cllr Jenny Simnett, was there to officially open it.

![Marlow FM's new mobile radio studio]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/news/new-trailer-at-regatta.jpg "Marlow FM's new mobile radio studio")

Our old trailer had served us well for many years, but no amount of sticky tape was going to hold it together for very much longer!

We were able to afford the new trailer thanks to a generous legacy from the late John Boot, a lifelong resident of Marlow and loyal listener to our community radio station.

Marlow FM founder Tim Ashburner put in many long hours to kit out the trailer and turn it into a swish radio studio - one with much needed extra space.

Look out for our new mobile studio at many local events throughout the rest of the year, and remember to come and say hello!
