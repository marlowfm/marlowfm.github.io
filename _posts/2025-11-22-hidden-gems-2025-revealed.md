Marlow's Hidden Gems of 2025 have been revealed. The winners were announced at an awards ceremony and party held at Harleyford Golf Club on Saturday 22nd November.

![Marlow's 2025 Hidden Gems revealed]({{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/logos/hidden-gems-2025-logo.jpg "Marlow's 2025 Hidden Gems revealed")

Congratulations to the winners below, but also to every person, business, and organisation nominated. Marlow is so lucky to have you all!

- **Best Cuppa** - The M Caf√©
- **Best Pub** - The Duke (Queen's Road)
- **Best Breakfast** - Fego
- **Best Event** - Marlow Bottom Scarecrow Trail
- **Best Night Out** - The Duke (Queen's Road)
- **Best Sports Club** - Marlow Rugby Club
- **Best Therapist** - Tree Nail Spa
- **Best Teacher** - Mrs Ambrose, Holy Trinity
- **Best Local Producer** - Rebellion Beer Company
- **Services to Charity** - The Link Foundation and Marlow Striders
- **Services to the Community** - Shelley Robson (The Barn Club) and Vicky and Graham Everitt (Chives)

A **Special Recognition Award** was also given to Hidden Gems organiser Pat Reading for all her work at the radio station and in the community.

More than 3,000 people voted when we announced the shortlists of nominees, after the public put forward nominations for each category.

Money raised from the event will go to help keep Marlow FM on air and serving the community.
