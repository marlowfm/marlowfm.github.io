Come and mingle with your fellow Marlovians at Vaasu by Atul Kochhar (2 Chapel Street, SL7 1DD) on Wednesday 25th March, and help raise money for Marlow FM.

It's an opportunity to network, meet old friends, and make new ones.

Tickets are £25 a head, and you'll enjoy local sparkling wine from Oaken Grove Vineyard in Fawley and delicious canapés from Vaasu.

And, if you can, stay and savour a two-course meal from Vaasu for an extra £25, payable separately on the day.

The event starts at 6pm, and dinner with be available from 8pm.

You can buy tickets by clicking the button below - please bring your receipt email along on paper or on your phone. And please contact Vaasu directly if you'd like to book a space for dinner - reservations@vaasurestaurant.co.uk or 020 3667 9990.
