If you live in or around Marlow, and are at least 18-years-old, you can get involved, whether as a presenter, or behind the scenes, or both. We don't always have slots immediately available, but we're still keen to hear from you if you'd like to join an existing programme, or if you've got an idea for a new one. You don't need any broadcasting experience - we can train you.

If you'd like to introduce yourself, please email info@marlowfm.co.uk.

Unfortunately, we can't, in general, offer work experience. As we have no full-time staff, there's usually nobody around all day to take someone under their wing. And, for safeguarding reasons, we don't allow anyone under the age of 18 to be in the studio without a guardian present.
