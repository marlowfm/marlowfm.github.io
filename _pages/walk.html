*Professor Greg Whyte OBE, a former Olympian and a sports scientist, has an impressive record of achievements. He represented Great Britain in the Modern Pentathlon at two Olympic Games and won European bronze and World Championship silver medals.

In addition to his athletic career, Greg has been involved in Comic Relief, where he trained and coached celebrities like David Walliams, John Bishop, James Cracknell, Cheryl Cole, Gary Barlow, and Eddie Izzard for charity challenges.

Greg is a Professor in Applied Sport and Exercise Science at Liverpool John Moores University. He is recognised as a UK authority on exercise physiology, sports performance, and rehabilitation.
