Spring into action with Marlow FM, and help us continue to raise money to kit out our wonderful new studios!

  • Professor Greg Whyte OBE*, Olympian and trainer to the stars, will lead the Marlow FM Night Walk on Saturday 11th May, starting with a warm-up at 7.30pm.
  • The half marathon walk will be made up of about 28 circuits of the field at Great Marlow School.
  • Entry and car parking at Redgrave Sports Centre, Wycombe Road, Marlow, SL7 3JD.
  • Dogs aren't allowed on the site. Sorry.
  • Tickets - £19.75 for adults, £9.75 for the under-18s, £50 for a family of four

Why not enter as a team and do the walk as a relay?

Do spread the word, and encourage friends and family to take part or sponsor you. We look forward to seeing you there!

*Professor Greg Whyte OBE, a former Olympian and a sports scientist, has an impressive record of achievements. He represented Great Britain in the Modern Pentathlon at two Olympic Games and won European bronze and World Championship silver medals.

In addition to his athletic career, Greg has been involved in Comic Relief, where he trained and coached celebrities like David Walliams, John Bishop, James Cracknell, Cheryl Cole, Gary Barlow, and Eddie Izzard for charity challenges.

Greg is a Professor in Applied Sport and Exercise Science at Liverpool John Moores University. He is recognised as a UK authority on exercise physiology, sports performance, and rehabilitation.
